Recommended Actions before play:
-------------------------------------------------

1. Ensure video card is of gtx 970 grade or higher
2. Update video drivers
3. Use Headphones for best experience
4. Play in dimly lit room to have best visual quality! The game is on the darker side

--------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------

1. Extract the contents of "v0.0.05.9.1.zip" to a directory of your choice.
2. Run "LegendsOfBorsippa.exe" to start the game.

--------------------------------------------------
How to Play:
--------------------------------------------------

Upon pressing the "Start Game" button, you will enter the sample level titled "The Tower: The Undercroft." 
This level serves as a demonstration of various game elements:

- Player movement
- Basic puzzle mechanics
- Combat system
- Player choices
- Atmospheric design

--------------------------------------------------
Player Controls:
--------------------------------------------------

- Movement: W, S, A, D keys
	- W: Forward
	- S: Backward
	- A: Left
	- D: Right
- Camera Control: Mouse inputs
- Sprint: Hold Shift key (consumes stamina)
- Dash: Space bar
- Equipment Management: 1, 3, 4, 5 keys
	- 1: Sword (Equip/Unequip)
	- 3: Shield (Equip/Unequip)
	- 4: Torch (Equip/Unequip)
	- 5: Bow (Equip/Unequip) (Cannot have any items equipped to equip)
- Combat:
  - Sword Swing: Left mouse button
  - Power Attack: Hold right mouse button, swing with left mouse button (sword equipped)
  - Shield Block: Hold right mouse button (shield equipped)
  - Torch Usage: Press right mouse button (torch equipped)
  - Bow: Hold right mouse button to prepare arrow, press left mouse button to fire the bow while arrow is prepared

Note: The torch does not cause damage to enemies in this version.

These controls enable you to explore, fight, solve puzzles, and progress through Legends of Borsippa. Have fun!
